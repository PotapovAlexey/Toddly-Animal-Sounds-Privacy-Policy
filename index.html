Privacy Policy

App: Toddly Animal Sounds
Developer: Black Lemon Gamings
Effective date: 2026-02-20

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Toddly Animal Sounds mobile application (the “App”).

Information We Collect

We collect limited, non-personal usage information to understand how the App is used and to improve it. This information may include:

  • App interactions (e.g., which sounds are played, taps on locked content)
  • Session information (e.g., app opens and session duration)
  • Approximate device and app information (e.g., device model, OS version, app version)

This information is collected using Google Firebase Analytics. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, photos, precise location, or any other directly identifying personal information.

How We Use Information

  • To understand usage trends and improve the App experience
  • To evaluate which content is most useful and improve navigation
  • To measure purchase funnel performance (without storing payment details)

In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases (for example, unlocking additional packs or “Unlock All”). Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your information. Usage data may be processed by Google as a service provider through Firebase Analytics. We share information only as necessary to provide and improve the App.

Children’s Privacy

The App may be used by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (for in-app purchases)
  • Firebase Analytics (for anonymous usage analytics)

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain analytics data for as long as necessary to support App improvement and reporting, according to Firebase settings and Google’s retention policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” above will reflect the latest version.
